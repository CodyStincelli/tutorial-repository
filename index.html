The Leviathan Clan Page

"A PvM Driven Clan"

Welcome to The Leviathan! We are a clan that likes to do every aspect in this game, but we do specialize in PvM. It is okay if you do not have much high level PvM experience because we are willing to teach if you have a good attitude. Our long term PvM goals are a Daredevil and a Bombi team. Everyone automatically gets admin rank and there is no requirement of capping at citadel. We hope everyone will eventually get completionist cape and will help you on you're journey there.

Feel free to use the resourses on this page whenever you want, the content will be kept up to date with the best methods and gear.

If you are interested in the clan feel free to reach out to anyone listed in the contacts page! Also check out our Rune Clan Page
