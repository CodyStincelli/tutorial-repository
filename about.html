About The Leviathan Clan

The Leviathan clan was created in January 2015, after and older clan fell apart. Since then we have been striving to create a helpful and fun clan. With every accepted member given access to avatar and no capping requirements. If you just want a helpful group of friend that have in-depth game knowledge then this is the clan for you.
